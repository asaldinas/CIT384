Our Mission

Every Matador deserves a beautiful campus. The CSUN Physical Plant Management Team is dedicated to creating a clean, safe, and welcoming environment so you can focus on learning, connecting, and thriving. Our goal is to make you proud to be a Matador every time you step on campus.

Grounds & Custodial Team

Behind every polished walkway, trimmed hedge, and spotless classroom is a team of professionals who care deeply about campus life. Our groundskeepers and custodial staff work early mornings, late nights, and everything in between to keep CSUN looking and feeling like home.

From seasonal landscaping projects to daily upkeep, their work ensures that every Matador can move through campus with comfort and pride.
